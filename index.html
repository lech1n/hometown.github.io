Visit Durres

Enjoy culture, history and beaches in this beautiful city in Albania.

Top three activities to do at Durres

Go shopping

In the shops near city center you can find local clothing brands that are very affordable and unique.

Go to the beach

The sandy shoreline reaches over 10 kilometers in length so you can find a beach everywhere.

Go to the museum

The museum exhibits a vast collection of artifacts from Dyrrhachium, the ancient name of Durres.
